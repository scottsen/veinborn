# Deep Dungeons & Destiny: Comprehensive Design Document

## 1. Core Systems

### 1.1 The Weave System

#### Essence Types
- **Elemental**
  - Fire (destruction, transformation)
  - Water (flow, adaptation)
  - Earth (stability, endurance)
  - Air (freedom, speed)
  - Lightning (energy, volatility)

- **Conceptual**
  - Time (speed, aging, prophecy)
  - Space (distance, teleportation)
  - Life (growth, healing)
  - Death (decay, necromancy)
  - Mind (consciousness, control)

- **Primal**
  - Light (illumination, truth)
  - Dark (shadow, secrecy)
  - Chaos (change, possibility)
  - Order (structure, stability)
  - Void (emptiness, potential)

#### Essence Interactions
```
Primary Combinations:
Fire + Air = Lightning
Water + Earth = Nature
Light + Dark = Twilight
Time + Space = Destiny
Life + Death = Cycle

Complex Combinations:
Fire + Time = Burning Speed
Water + Mind = Memory Flow
Void + Light = Star Power
Dark + Life = Shadow Growth
```

#### Essence Collection
- Defeat monsters to collect their innate essence
- Find essence nodes in the environment
- Extract essence from magical items
- Discover rare pure essence crystals
- Combine lesser essences into greater forms

## 3. Combat Depth

```

Status Effects:

Mystical:

## 5. The Deep Game

### 5.1 System Interactions

#### Example Combinations
```

-> Later exposed to Time = Blade of Eternal Flame

## 6. Design Principles

### Core Philosophies
1. Every death teaches something
2. All systems interact meaningfully
3. Discovery drives progression
4. Knowledge equals power
5. Simple rules, complex emergence
6. No meaningless choices
7. Always something new to learn
8. Perfect mastery is always possible
9. Every run is unique
10. The game grows with the player

### The Final Truth
Through mastery of materials, essences, and weapons, players eventually understand the true nature of the dungeon - a living entity of pure potential, seeking someone capable of understanding and reshaping reality itself. Each run, each death, each discovery is part of this entity testing and teaching those who enter, preparing them for the ultimate responsibility of cosmic creation.
